Latino Business Student Association

In the spring 2024 semester, I decided to join the Latino Business Student Association. Initially nervous because I didn't know anyone in the club, I was pleasantly surprised by how welcoming everyone was. While I've always been busy with my side businesses and other aspects of life, I realized the importance of stepping out of my comfort zone to make friends and build meaningful relationships.

Joining LBSA has been one of the best decisions I've made since transferring to SJSU. Although I joined late in the semester and couldn't attend all the events, I made sure to participate in as many as possible. Through LBSA, I've had incredible opportunities to visit different companies, meet industry professionals, and connect with students from neighboring LBSA programs. It's been a meaningful experience getting more in touch with my fellow Latino brothers and sisters.

I encourage everyone, regardless of ethnicity, to join! It's a fantastic way to expand your network, gain valuable insights, and build lasting friendships.

Management Information Systems Association

MISA was the first club I joined after transferring to SJSU in the fall of 2023. However, during my first semester, I didn't attend as much as I would have liked. Adjusting to a new school, moving away from my hometown, and living independently for the first time all contributed to a bit of culture shock.

In Spring 2024, I decided to fully engage with MISA by attending their company tours and career workshops. These experiences were instrumental in enhancing my interviewing skills, refining my resume formatting, and gaining insights from informative presentations by industry professionals and leaders. MISA has proven to be a vital club at SJSU for MIS students, offering opportunities to build practical skills directly applicable in the real world. This active participation not only helped me overcome initial challenges but also significantly contributed to my personal and professional growth at SJSU.
