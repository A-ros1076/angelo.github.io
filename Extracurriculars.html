Northstar Photonics, a company with fewer than 50 employees creating cutting-edge technology, has provided me with an incredible internship experience thus far (as of 7/1/24). Being their first intern and the youngest by more than two decades, I initially felt quite nervous.

I joined NSP as a supply chain procurement intern, in my first week I was immersed in the field of photonics, working on a new project with numerous moving parts. During this time, I gained extensive knowledge and was deeply involved in creating bills of materials, workflow charts, planning project schedules and facilitating communication across all departments, it felt amazing to actually contribute to something using the exact skills I had been learning in the previous semester!

I was able to sit in on executive meetings with my manager, observing how high level managers plan, track and execute, this was invaluable and has been one of the biggest learning experiences to date. The dedication to communication, relationship building, leadership and curiosity was exemplified in every meeting I attended, highlighting how vital these skills are across all industries.

While my internship is only half way completed, I learn something new everyday. Wheater it's presenting to the CEO/COO on a project or identifying and solving issues with vendors to maintain production schedules, this experience has provided me with knowledge that is not obtainable in the classroom and I am greatful everyday to be apart of such a fun, innovative and creative company.
